Jolly's Gulch

Location

Zion Nation Park. Off the west rim trail, or can be accessed off of Zion Ponderosa Ranch property.

Description

Very fun, short canyon. Only 2 rappels, but both are big and fun. Can be done when running with water, but it's rare. Popular hiking spot so be sure to clean your anchors. Permits required through the national park.

Conditions

Almost always dry. Can be running in the spring.
